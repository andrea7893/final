Materials used

- two 4mm yarns of different colors
- 4-5mm crochet hook

Pouch

- slip knot
- 41 chains
- row 1: skip chain 1, sc x5, change color, sc x5, repeat
- at the end of row 1, chain 1 and turn
- row 2: repeat row 1 until end of row 5
- at the end of row 5, switch color, chain 1, turn
- row 6: repeat pattern
- after finishing the rows, single crochet the border color on all sides

Strap

- 171 chains
- row 1-5: make 34 squares
-single crochet the border color on each side

Assembly

- slip stitch both sides together
